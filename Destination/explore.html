Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the real-time data?

Our real-time data is updated every 5-15 minutes depending on the source. We aggregate information from multiple reliable sources including official APIs, user reports, and IoT sensors deployed in popular tourist areas. While we strive for maximum accuracy, we recommend using the information as a guide rather than absolute truth.

Can I use the maps offline?

Yes! Our platform allows you to download map sections for offline use. While real-time features will not be available offline, you can still access basic map functionality, saved points of interest, and pre-downloaded local insights. This feature is especially useful when traveling in areas with limited connectivity.

How do I contribute local insights?

Registered users can contribute local insights by selecting "Add Insight" on any location. Your contribution will be reviewed by our community managers and, once approved, will be available to all users. Regular contributors with high-quality insights may earn "Local Expert" status, giving their recommendations higher visibility.

Is the platform available worldwide?

Our platform currently covers major tourist destinations across North America, Europe, and parts of Asia. We're rapidly expanding to include more regions, with a goal of global coverage by next year. Even in less-covered areas, basic map functionality and user-contributed insights may be available.
